Kimetsu no Yaiba

History

The story follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted and intelligent boy who lives with his family, and earns money by selling charcoal. Everything changes when his family is killed by a demon. His sister Niziko Kamado is the only survivor of the accident but turned into a demon. Tanjiro became a demon slayer to help his sister, avenging the death of his family. More Details
